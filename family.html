Moana of Motuni

Moana is a strong-willed, independent wayfinder. Though she has moments of self-doubt, she has great pride in who she is and where she comes from, and doesn't back away from new challenges.Moana, the strong-willed daughter of a chief of a coastal village, who is chosen by the ocean itself to reunite a mystical relic with the goddess Te Fiti.

Family

  • Sina

    • Moana's mother, Tui's wife, and Chieftess of Motunui.

    • Sina always has her daughter's back. Playful, sharp and strong-willed, Sina appreciates Moana's longing to be on the water, but also wants to protect her daughter from the fabled dangers beyond the reef


  • Tui

    • Moana's overprotective father, Sina's husband, and Tala's son. He is chief of Motunui Island

    • Tui was an aspiring voyager in his youth, but after experiencing a traumatic event, developed aquaphobia and forbade his people from journeying beyond his island's reef.


  • Simea

    • a rambunctious 3-and-a-half-year-old who wants to be just like her big sister.

    • Simea is Moana's mini, She is cute and energetic. She's a kindred spirit to Moana, She's not okay with her sister leaving, but then she knows she's Moana and she has to go. So, she still gives her that drive.


  • Gramma Tala

    • She is the mother of Tui, the mother-in-law of Sina and the grandmother of Moana and Simea.

    • Proud to be the village crazy lady,When Gramma Tala dies, she comes back as a manta ray, just like the one in her tattoo. Gramma Tala's clothes also have manta ray patterns on them.
