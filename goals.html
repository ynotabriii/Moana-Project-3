Moana of Motuni

Moana is a strong-willed, independent wayfinder. Though she has moments of self-doubt, she has great pride in who she is and where she comes from, and doesn't back away from new challenges.Moana, the strong-willed daughter of a chief of a coastal village, who is chosen by the ocean itself to reunite a mystical relic with the goddess Te Fiti.

Goals

  • Main Goal

    • Moana goes on a mission to find Maui, the demi-god, in order to restore the heart of the ocean. Although Maui constantly picks on Moana by treating her like a child, she stays adamant on her mission to restore the heart.

  • Goals Throughout the Movie

    • Since moana was a baby she was drawn to the ocean. Through the movie she is constantly finding a way to get back to the ocean.

    • As moana got older she was determined to make it pass the reef successfully, and wants to push boundaries. She wants to go beyond her society's current knowledge.

    • When moana realized what she needed to do she was set on restoring the heart, even though she did not know that different monsters were involved her goal was to defeat them in some way and make it across the ocean.

    • Healing Te Ka/Te Fiti also works to carry out a number of Moana's deepest desires/goals: to bring healing to her island, to help her people, to partner with the ocean, and be a voyager. The healing of Te Ka not only healed Te Ka and her island, but also brought Moana purpose.
